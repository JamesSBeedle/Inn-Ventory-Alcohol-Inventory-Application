How to use the application

Select the area of the application you wish to access using the buttons below.

You are able view, add, edit and delete both products and suppliers.

If the product or supplier is missing from the view all section you can add them using the Add button at the top of the page.

To view a single product or supplier just click the view button on their relevant entry.

To edit or delete a product or supplier click the view button on the relevant entry and then click the edit or delete button as required.
